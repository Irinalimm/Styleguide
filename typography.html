Typography

Our app's typography is built on two primary typefaces: Montserrat and Noto Sans.
Montserrat is used for headings to capture attention with its geometric elegance and modern flair, while Noto Sans is employed for body text, ensuring readability and comfort for extended reading. This combination provides a balanced visual hierarchy, enhancing the user experience through clear and consistent typography.

Montserrat

Reasoning

Montserrat offers a geometric, clean aesthetic that's great for headings and stand-out text, giving the app a modern and uncluttered look.
